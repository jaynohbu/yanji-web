Simone Mongini is the founder of London Branding & Digital Design company MonginiGraphics.
MonginiGraphics' leading aim is to create beautiful Graphics & Websites through an emotional connection.

Growing up around Lake Maggiore in northern Italy, Simone studied graphic design at NABA in Milan.
He then learnt how to code himself and he has now been liaising with design projects for over 10 years.

WHAT MAKES
MY
PROJECTS REMARKABLE:

  • RESPONSIVE LAYOUT

    No matter what device you are browsing with, your website will look stunning everywhere by learning how to adapt and rearrange its content.

  • EXCLUSIVE GRAPHICS

    It's not "just another" ready-made template. It's a fearless bespoke website built from ground-up to make you stand out from the samey crowd.

  • USER-FRIENDLY

    Progressive disclosure is the key to future web design. I always achieve an excellent user experience by reducing the clutter and confusion.

  • CONTENT QUALITY

    Colour balance, interactivity, performance, call to actions and much more. These are fundamental features in every work I put my hands on.

  • COMPELLING NARRATIVE

    Powerful visuals are always partnered with a strong narrative. The headings walk you through the site and make you want to find out more.

  • PIXEL PERFECTION

    Pixels are gauged to emphasize every aspect of the story. All elements are always implemented to the fullest, for the sharpest, cleanest look.
